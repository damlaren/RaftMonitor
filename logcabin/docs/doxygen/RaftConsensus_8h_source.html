751 /**
752  * An implementation of the Raft consensus algorithm. The algorithm is
753  * described at https://ramcloud.stanford.edu/raft.pdf
754  * . In brief, Raft divides time into terms and elects a leader at the
755  * beginning of each term. This election mechanism guarantees that the emerging
756  * leader has at least all committed log entries. Once a candidate has received
757  * votes from a quorum, it replicates its own log entries in order to the
758  * followers. The leader is the only machine that serves client requests.
759  */
